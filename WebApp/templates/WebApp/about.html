About Our Project

We were tasked with creating a web page using the Django system and following the Kanban methodology to encourage sustainability efforts on campus at the University of Exeter. We initially brainstormed many ideas for how we could use gamification principles to create an engaging web page that would be fun and keep users coming back.

We ultimately decided on using a pet-based challenge system, where users complete challenges to feed and "level up" their pet. This approach allows users to feel rewarded as they watch their pet grow and fosters collaboration as they compare pets. Customization of pets is tied to completing challenges, providing further motivation.

In line with the eco-friendly theme and the concept of a pet "growing," we chose a green bean themed pet.
