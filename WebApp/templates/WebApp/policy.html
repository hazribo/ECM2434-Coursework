Climate Policy

Introduction

Our project is in line with the University Of Exeter goals for increasing sustainability on campus. Specifically,

  • - Helping the university achieve carbon net zero by 2030
  • - Encouraging users to act in line with the UN's Sustainable Development Goals
  • - Encouraging users to visit parts of campus that promote biodiversity

    • Achieving Carbon Net Zero

    As students and staff using the University campus, we contribute to its carbon emissions, specifically for scope 3 emissions (carbon emissions created by users of the campus). While the university works to reduce scope 1 and scope 2 emissions (carbon emissions created by the university and its partnerships), we must help reduce our own emissions as our part of preventing climate change.

    The best way we can reduce our carbon emissions is by reducing our carbon footprint. By engaging with our Green Beans project, missions are designed so that you slowly reduce your emissions simply by participating with the game and levelling up your pet. We also encourage users to use the WWF's carbon footprint calculator to gain additional advice, and watch as your footprint is reduced by engaging with your Bean Pet!
